MVP

(Most Viable Product) is software which helps users to analyze the product, assess its functionality and quality, and help users choose the right product for themselves. MVP software analyzes the usage requirements or the prerequisites for the product from the user through a simple questionnaire and lists down the various products that fit the user requirements, further analyzing and choosing the best product for the customer.
